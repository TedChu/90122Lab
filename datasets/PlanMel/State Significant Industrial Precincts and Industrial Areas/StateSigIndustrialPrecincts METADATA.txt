Title: State Significant Industrial Precincts

Description: Polygons showing future and existing State Significant Industrial Precincts. Existing State Significant Industrial Precincts have been derived from Planning Scheme industrial zones in July 2016. Future State Significant Industrial Precincts are derived from land identified for industry in the Growth Corridor Plans 2012 (MPA). 

Source: DELWP - Plan Melbourne

Data Date: July 2016
